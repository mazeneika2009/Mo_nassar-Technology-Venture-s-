Getting Started

How do I create a new project? ‚ñº
To create a new project, click on the "Projects" link in the navigation menu or use the "Create New Project" button on the dashboard. Fill in the project details like name, category, and template, then click "Create Project".
How do I invite team members? ‚ñº
You can invite team members by navigating to the Team section or clicking "Manage Team" on the dashboard. Enter the email addresses of the people you want to invite and assign them roles (Team Lead, Member, or Viewer). They will receive an invitation email.
What are the different project templates? ‚ñº
TaskFlow offers three project templates: Kanban Board (for workflow management), Timeline/Gantt (for project scheduling), and Checklist (for simple task tracking). Choose the one that best fits your project needs.

Managing Projects

How do I add tasks to my project? ‚ñº
Click on "Add Tasks" in the Quick Actions section of the dashboard. Enter the task title, description, assign it to a team member, set a due date, and specify the priority level. Click "Add Task" to save it to your project.
Can I change project privacy settings? ‚ñº
Yes, you can set your project as either Private (only invited members can view) or Public (anyone can view but only members can edit). You can change this setting anytime by editing your project details.
How do I edit or delete a project? ‚ñº
Go to your project and click "Edit Project" to modify its details. To delete a project, open the project details modal and click the delete option. This action cannot be undone, so please be careful.

Team Collaboration

What are the different team member roles? ‚ñº
TaskFlow has three roles: Team Lead (full access and can manage team), Member (can create and edit tasks), and Viewer (can only view tasks). Assign roles based on your team's needs.
How do I remove a team member? ‚ñº
Go to the Team Members section on the dashboard and click on the member you want to remove. Click the "Remove Member" button to revoke their access to the project.
